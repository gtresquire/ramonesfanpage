Bio..

The Ramones formed in 1975 in New York City, playing their earliest shows at the legendary bar and music venue CBGS. Though the band achieved modest success during their lifetimes they are now widely acknowledged as one of the greatest and most original rock bands of all time. The Ramones' style broke free of the stadium rock and disco music that was dominating the airwaves in the mid 1970s bringing a raw energy evocative of rocks & roll's early days, while originating a new style of music that would become known as punk rock. Their influence continues to this day and their spirit lives on. Tommy, Johnny, Joey, Dee Dee - The Ramones.
Bio from 1975 as written by Tommy Ramone
